# Childcare Center - Admission & Enrollment FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions About Admissions

### 1. What is the admission process?

**Answer:** Our admission process involves several steps:

**Step 1: Initial Inquiry**
- Contact us via phone, email, or website
- Schedule a center tour
- Receive information packet

**Step 2: Center Tour**
- Visit our facility
- Meet our staff
- See classrooms and play areas
- Ask questions

**Step 3: Application**
- Complete enrollment application
- Provide required documents
- Pay registration fee

**Step 4: Enrollment**
- Receive acceptance notification
- Complete enrollment paperwork
- Schedule orientation session
- Set start date

**Timeline:** Typically 1-2 weeks from application to enrollment

### 9. What is your policy on toilet training?

**Answer:** We support families through the toilet training process.

**Our Approach:**
- Child-led readiness
- Partnership with parents
- Positive reinforcement
- No pressure or negative consequences

**Toilet Training Protocol:**

**Readiness Signs:**
- Stays dry for 2+ hours
- Shows interest in using toilet
- Can pull pants up/down
- Communicates need to go

**Parent Preparation:**
- Discuss readiness with teacher
- Establish consistent routine at home
- Provide multiple changes of clothes
- Send wipes and training pants

**At Center:**
- Regular bathroom breaks
- Encourage but don't force
- Accidents handled calmly
- Daily communication with parents

**Timeline:**
- Start when child shows readiness
- Usually age 2-3 years
- Takes 3-6 months typically
- No rush or pressure

**Not Required For:**
- Transitioning to toddler room
- Starting preschool (though usually trained by 3.5)
