### 2. Will you give my child medication?

**Answer:** Yes, with proper authorization:

**Prescription Medication:**
- Doctor's written authorization
- Original pharmacy label
- Specific dosing instructions
- Parent permission form
- Logged each dose

**Over-the-Counter Medication:**
- Doctor's note required
- Parent authorization
- Original packaging
- No aspirin to children

**We Can Administer:**
- Oral medications
- Topical creams (diaper rash, etc.)
- Eye/ear drops
- Inhalers (with spacer)
- Epi-pens

**We Cannot:**
- Give medication without authorization
- Adjust dosages
- Share medication between children
- Accept expired medication

**Process:**
1. Bring medication in original container
2. Complete medication form
3. Hand directly to staff (never leave in bag)
4. Staff administers and logs
5. Daily report shows administration

### 3. Do you require immunizations?

**Answer:** Yes, current immunizations required:

**Required Vaccinations:**
- DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis)
- IPV (Polio)
- MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
- Hib (Haemophilus influenzae type b)
- Hepatitis A & B
- Varicella (Chickenpox)
- Pneumococcal
- Flu vaccine (recommended annually)
- COVID-19 (if age-eligible, per state requirements)

**Documentation:**
- Current immunization record
- Signed by healthcare provider
- Updated as child receives new vaccines
- Must be current to attend

**Exemptions:**
- Medical: Doctor's note required
- Religious/Philosophical: Affidavit required (if state allows)
- Families notified if unvaccinated child in class

**Grace Period:**
- 30 days to provide records for new students
- Provisional enrollment
- Cannot attend after grace period without records

### 4. What happens if my child gets injured?

**Answer:** Immediate care and parent notification:

**Minor Injuries:**
- First aid administered
- Ice pack, band-aid, comfort
- Incident report completed
- Parent notified at pick-up
- Report sent home

**Moderate Injuries:**
- First aid provided
- Parent called immediately
- May recommend doctor visit
- Detailed incident report
- Follow-up next day

**Serious Injuries:**
- 911 called immediately
- First aid/CPR if needed
- Parent/emergency contact called
- Staff accompanies child if parent can't
- Incident report + investigation

**Documentation:**
- All injuries documented
- Photos if visible mark
- Witnesses noted
- Action taken recorded
- Parent signature required

**Prevention:**
- Regular safety checks
- Age-appropriate equipment
- Constant supervision
- Safety training

### 7. What if there's a COVID or flu outbreak?

**Answer:** Immediate communication and protocols:

**If Case Identified:**
- Families notified immediately (anonymously)
- Deep cleaning of affected areas
- Increased sanitization
- Monitor for symptoms
- Follow health department guidelines

**Exposure Protocol:**
- Close contacts identified
- Parents notified
- Testing recommended
- Quarantine if required
- Virtual options discussed

**Center Closure:**
- Health department mandated
- Multiple cases in short time
- Staff shortages due to illness
- Deep sanitization needed

**Communication:**
- Email/text alerts
- Daily updates
- Transparent information
- Follow CDC/state guidelines

**Prevention:**
- Daily health screenings
- Enhanced cleaning
- Hand hygiene
- Ventilation
- Sick policy enforcement

### 10. What cleaning and sanitation procedures do you follow?

**Answer:** Rigorous cleaning protocols:

**Daily Cleaning:**
- Surfaces sanitized (tables, chairs, doorknobs)
- Toys cleaned and sanitized
- Bathrooms cleaned 2x daily
- Floors vacuumed/mopped
- Trash removed

**Toys:**
- Mouthed toys: Removed and sanitized daily
- Other toys: Rotated and cleaned weekly
- Stuffed animals: Laundered weekly
- Outdoor toys: Weekly cleaning

**Diaper Area:**
- Sanitized after each change
- Disposable covers used
- Step-by-step hygiene protocol
- Hand washing required

**Kitchen:**
- Food prep surfaces sanitized
- Dishes sanitized (high-temp dishwasher)
- Refrigerator cleaned weekly
- Food safety protocols

**Deep Cleaning:**
- Nightly by cleaning service
- Windows, vents, deep surfaces
- Carpets shampooed monthly
- Quarterly deep clean

**Products Used:**
- EPA-approved disinfectants
- Child-safe when dry
- Following manufacturer instructions
- No harsh chemicals around children

**Hand Hygiene:**
- Before eating
- After bathroom
- After outdoor play
- Before/after sensory play
- Staff and children
