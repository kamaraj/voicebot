# Childcare Center - Hours & Schedule FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions About Operating Hours and Schedules

### 1. What are your operating hours?

**Answer:** We operate year-round with the following schedule:

**Regular Hours:**
- **Monday - Friday:** 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Saturday:** Closed
- **Sunday:** Closed

**Extended Hours (if available):**
- Early Drop-off: 6:30 AM (additional fee)
- Late Pick-up: Until 6:30 PM (additional fee)

**Holiday Schedule:**
- See annual calendar for specific closures
- Typically closed 8-10 days per year
- Notice provided 30 days in advance

---

### 2. What time should I drop off and pick up my child?

**Answer:** Flexible within operating hours:

**Drop-off:**
- **Anytime:** 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM (preferred)
- **Late arrivals:** Please call ahead
- **Sign-in required:** At front desk

**Pick-up:**
- **Anytime:** 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- **Before 6:00 PM sharp:** Late fees apply after
- **Sign-out required:** Photo ID may be requested

**Best Practices:**
- Consistent schedule helps child's routine
- Notify staff if running late
- Call if not arriving by usual time
- Emergency contacts used if unreachable

**Answer:** We observe the following holidays:

**Major Holidays (Closed):**

- Memorial Day

---

### 4. What happens if I'm late for pick-up?

**Answer:** We understand emergencies happen:

**Late Pick-up Policy:**
- **After 6:00 PM:** $1 per minute
- **After 6:15 PM:** $2 per minute
- **After 6:30 PM:** Emergency contacts called

**Example:**
- Pick-up at 6:10 PM = $10 late fee
- Pick-up at 6:25 PM = $45 late fee
- Charged to your account automatically

**Emergency Situations:**
- Call immediately if running late
- We'll comfort your child
- Emergency contacts used if unreachable after 30 min
- Repeated tardiness may affect enrollment

**Tips to Avoid:**
- Leave work with buffer time
- Have backup person authorized
- Update emergency contacts
- Set phone reminders

---

### 5. Can I change my child's schedule?

**Answer:** Yes, with proper notice:

**Permanent Changes:**
- 2 weeks written notice required
- Subject to availability
- May affect tuition rate
- Prorated from change date

**Temporary Changes:**
- Extra days: 24 hours notice (if space available)
- Fewer days: No credit unless 2+ weeks
- Time changes: Communicate with teacher

**Summer Schedule:**
- Can adjust for vacation
- Must maintain minimum enrollment
- School-age care available

**Process:**
1. Submit schedule change form
2. Wait for approval
3. Receive confirmation
4. New schedule effective next billing period

### 9. What if there's bad weather or emergency?

**Answer:** Safety is priority - here's our protocol:

**Weather Closures:**
- **Decision by:** 6:00 AM day of
- **Notifications via:**
  - Email blast
  - Text message
  - Phone call
  - Website/social media
  - Local news

**Snow Days:**
- Close if roads unsafe
- Follow local emergency management
- Full tuition still applies

**Other Emergencies:**
- Fire alarm: Evacuate to designated area
- Severe weather: Shelter in place
- Medical emergency: 911 + parent notification
- Security threat: Lockdown procedures

**Emergency Communication:**
- Keep contact info current
- Answer emergency calls
- Check email during weather events

**Make-up Days:**
- No make-up for weather closures
- Tuition holds your spot
- Safety takes precedence

### 10. Can I visit during the day?

**Answer:** Yes! Open door policy with some guidelines:

**Visiting Hours:**
- **Anytime during operating hours**
- No appointment needed for enrolled families
- Prospective families: Please schedule tour

**Visit Purposes:**
- Lunch with your child
- Read a story to class
- Observe your child
- Special events
- Volunteer activities

**Guidelines:**
- Sign in at front desk
- Wear visitor badge
- Respect nap times (observe quietly)
- Don't bring sick children/visitors
- Limit visit duration if child gets upset

**Special Visit Days:**
- Parents' Day: Monthly lunch
- Grandparents' Day: Annual
- Career Day: Share your job
- Read to Class: Sign up

**Classroom Disruption:**
- Teachers may request
brief visits if: 
  - Child having difficult day
  - Transition period
  - Testing time
  - Special activity in progress

**We Welcome:**
- Surprise lunch visits
- Reading to class
- Sharing your culture/profession
- Being involved!
