# Childcare Center - Fees & Payment FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions About Fees and Payments

### 1. What are your tuition rates?

**Answer:** Our tuition rates vary by age group and enrollment type:

**Full-Time Rates (Monday-Friday, 7 AM - 6 PM):**
- **Infants (6 weeks - 18 months):** $1,200 - $1,500/month
- **Toddlers (18 months - 3 years):** $1,000 - $1,300/month
- **Preschool (3 - 5 years):** $900 - $1,200/month
- **After-School (5 - 12 years):** $600 - $800/month

**Part-Time Rates:**
- **Half-Day (4-5 hours):** 60% of full-time rate
- **3 Days/Week:** 70% of full-time rate
- **2 Days/Week:** 50% of full-time rate

**Additional Fees:**
- Registration Fee: $150 (one-time, non-refundable)
- Annual Materials Fee: $100
- Late Pick-up: $1 per minute after 6 PM
- Drop-in Care: $15-$20/hour (when available)

*Rates subject to change annually. Sibling discounts available.*

### 3. When is tuition due?

**Answer:** Tuition payment schedule and deadlines:

**Payment Frequency:**
- **Monthly:** Due on the 1st of each month
- **Bi-Weekly:** Every other Friday
- **Weekly:** Every Monday (if arranged)

**Due Dates:**
- Payment must be received by 5th of month
- Considered late after 5PM on due date
- Late fee applied after grace period

**Advance Payment:**
- First month's tuition due upon enrollment
- Last month's tuition held as security deposit
- Prorated for mid-month starts

**Holiday Months:**
- Full tuition still applies
- No reduction for closed days
- Tuition reserves your child's spot

**Summer/Break:**
- Full tuition maintains enrollment
- Option to pause with advanced notice (subject to re-enrollment fee)

### 4. What is your late payment policy?

**Answer:** We understand occasional delays but have policies to maintain operations:

**Late Payment Consequences:**

**Day 1-5 (Grace Period):**
- Reminder email sent
- No late fee
- Please communicate if payment delayed

**Day 6-10:**
- $25 late fee applied
- Second reminder sent
- Payment required to continue care

**Day 11-15:**
- $50 total late fee
- Account placed on hold
- Meeting with director required

**After Day 15:**
- Care may be suspended
- Immediate payment required for return
- May lose enrollment spot
- Collections process may begin

**Communication is Key:**
- Call/email if payment will be late
- Payment plans available in hardship
- We want to work with families!

**Autopay Benefits:**
- Never late
- No late fees
- Peace of mind

### 5. Do you offer sibling discounts?

**Answer:** Yes! We offer discounts for multiple children:

**Sibling Discount Structure:**
- **Second Child:** 10% off tuition
- **Third Child:** 15% off tuition
- **Fourth+ Child:** 20% off tuition

**How It Works:**
- Discount applied to younger child(ren)
- Oldest child pays full rate
- Automatic calculation in billing

**Example:**
```
Child 1 (Preschool): $1,000/month (full price)
Child 2 (Toddler): $1,170/month $1,300 - 10%
Child 3 (Infant): $1,275/month ($1,500 - 15%)
Total: $3,445/month (savings of $525!)
```

**Other Discounts:**
- Military/First Responder: 5%
- Referral Bonus: $100 credit
- Annual Pre-payment: 5% discount
- Teacher/Staff Children: 25% off

*Discounts cannot be combined. Maximum discount 25%.*

### 8. What is your refund and withdrawal policy?

**Answer:** Our policies regarding refunds and withdrawal:

**Withdrawal Notice:**
- **Required Notice:** 30 days written notice
- **Notice Date:** From date received, not end of month
- **Tuition During Notice:** Full payment required

**Example:**
- Notice given May 15th
- Last day: June 15th
- May tuition: Full month
- June tuition: Full month
- Prorated only with emergency circumstances

**Refund Policy:**

**Non-Refundable:**
- Registration fee
- Supply fees
- Past services rendered
- Advance payments applied to notice period

**Refundable:**
- Security deposit (last month's tuition)
- Unused prepaid field trips
- Unused enrichment programs

**Refund Timeline:**
- Security deposit returned within 30 days of last day
- Deductions for damages, unpaid fees, etc.
- Check or direct deposit

**Vacation Credit:**
- No credit for vacations under 2 weeks
- Credit possible for absences over 2 consecutive weeks with advance notice
- Maximum 2 weeks credit per year

**Emergency Withdrawal:**
- Job loss, relocation, medical emergency
- Meet with director
- May waive notice period
- Case-by-case basis

### 10. Can I pay tuition annually or in advance?

**Answer:** Yes, we offer advance and annual payment options:

**Annual Prepayment:**
- **Discount:** 5% off annual tuition
- **Payment Due:** Beginning of school year (September)
- **Amount:** 12 months tuition minus 5%
- **Non-Refundable:** After 30 days

**Example Savings:**
- Monthly tuition: $1,000
- Annual cost: $12,000
- Prepayment: $11,400
- **Savings: $600 per year!**

**Multi-Month Prepayment:**
- Pay 3, 6, or 9 months in advance
- Discount: 2-3% depending on duration
- More flexible than annual

**Benefits of Advance Payment:**
- Cost savings
- Budget certainty
- One transaction per year
- No late payment risk
- Priority enrollment

**Considerations:**
- Large upfront cost
- Limited refund if withdraw early
- Still responsible for additional fees

**Partial Refund Policy:**
- If withdraw, unused months prorated
- Minus withdrawn discount
- After 30 days only

**How to Set Up:**
1. Contact billing department
2. Receive annual invoice
3. Choose payment method
4. Receive discount confirmation
5. Confirmation of credit on account

**We also accept:**
- 529 College Savings Plans (childcare portion)
- Gift payments from grandparents/family
- Estate planning gifts
